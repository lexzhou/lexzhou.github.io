Hi there 👋, I am Lexin. I am a Master's candidate in ML at the [University of Cambridge](https://www.cam.ac.uk/) and Data Science graduate at [TU Valencia](http://www.upv.es/). I work on AI Evaluation and AI Predictability research at [VRAIN](https://vrain.upv.es/), advised by Prof. [José Hernández-Orallo](http://josephorallo.webs.upv.es/). I will soon be starting a summer research internship at [Krueger Lab](https://www.davidscottkrueger.com/) in Cambridge, focusing on Scalable Oversight and Alignment, advised by [Gabriel Recchia](http://www.twonewthings.com/gabrielrecchia/) and Prof. [José Hernández-Orallo](http://josephorallo.webs.upv.es/). I also dedicate a slight proportion of my time to some contract work at [Meta AI](https://ai.facebook.com/) and the [European Commission JRC](https://joint-research-centre.ec.europa.eu/jrc-sites-across-europe_en). My bit is to make AI more socially beneficial, better evaluate the systems we build, anticipate what they are capable of, and deploy them safely. My main research agenda encompasses several areas of Artificial Intelligence, Evaluation of AI, AI Safety, AI Predictability, AI Alignment, Scalable Oversight, General Purpose AI, and Foundation Models.
